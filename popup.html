How to use Circle to Search:

  1. Click "Start Circling" or the extension icon
  2. Draw a circle around anything on the webpage
  3. Get instant search results and information
  4. Press ESC to exit circle mode

Tips:

  • Circle text, images, or any content
  • Works on any webpage
  • Larger circles capture more content
  • Results appear automatically
